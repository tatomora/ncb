Enhanced Protection Against Fraud

Your new NCB Mastercard Black credit card gives you advanced security every time you shop, thanks to the power of EMV Chip and PIN technology. The chip that is embedded in your card acts as a micro-computer which stores and protects your valuable information. Each time you use your card in-store or at an ABM, a unique number will be generated. This number will change every time you use your card, making it almost impossible for your chip card to be counterfeited.
